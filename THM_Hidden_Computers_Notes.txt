ROOM: SOPHIA'S SUMMER OF HIDDEN COMPUTERS - NOTES
==================================================

**Task 1: Introduction**
- Computers sirf laptop/desktop nahi hote
- Roz marra ki cheezon mein "hidden computers" hote hain
- Goal: Real life mein computers ko pehchanna

**Task 2: Sophia's Summer - Month 1**
- Example 1: Microwave
    - Small computer = microcontroller
    - Kaam: Time set karna, power control karna
- Example 2: Washing Machine  
    - Sensors: Water level, weight detect karte hain
    - Programs: Different kapdon ke liye alag settings
- Key Point: Ye devices input leti hain, process karti hain, output deti hain

**Task 3: Sophia's Summer - Month 2**  
- Example 3: Car/ECU (Engine Control Unit)
    - Kaam: Fuel injection, air ratio, engine timing control
    - Input: Pedal sensor, temperature sensor
- Example 4: Smart Fridge
    - Kaam: Temperature maintain, expiry track karna
    - Internet se connect ho kar groceries order kar sakta
- Key Point: "Embedded Systems" kehlate hain - specific kaam ke liye bane computers

**Task 4: Why Computers Come in Different Flavors**
- General Purpose: Laptop/Desktop
    - Kaam: Har tarah ka kaam kar sakte - net, typing, games
    - Powerful but zyada bijli khate hain
- Special Purpose / Embedded: Microwave, Car ECU
    - Kaam: Sirf 1 kaam karte hain but bahut ache se
    - Saste, kam bijli, chote hote hain
- Reason: Har kaam ke liye al
